RUNEBIT DICE: ARCANE RUNES D20, FREE SAMPLE
===========================================

Version 1.0

Photorealistic transparent-PNG D20 result sprites with an obsidian body wreathed in forking electric-blue lightning veins and bright cyan-etched numerals.
Free sample showing all 20 result faces of the D20.

This is a free companion to the **Runebit Dice: Arcane Runes Complete
Polyhedral Result Sprite Pack** (D4-D20+D% in the same theme).
The paid polyhedral pack ships in the next few days on itch.io; follow runebitdice on itch for the drop.

Contents
--------

- 20 D20 result sprites (one per face)
- Sizes included: 1024 px, 512 px, 256 px (all square, transparent background)
- Render style: Cycles path-traced, carved-numeral 3D geometry with
  emissive Roman-inscription (Cinzel Bold) glyphs and forking lightning-vein obsidian material.

Reading die results:

The numeral centered on each visible face is the rolled value, 1 through 20.
