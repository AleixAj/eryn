CHANGELOG
=========

v1.0, 2026-05-11
----------------

Initial release.

- 20 D20 result sprites at 1024 / 512 / 256 px.
- Theme: Nightfall Marble (a near-black blue-tinted marble body with deep blue-violet veins, pale silver-white sparkle peaks, and gold inset-etch numerals carved into the polished stone).
- Font: Barlow Bold (SIL OFL, used only to generate numeral geometry).
- Camera framing: consistent three-quarter overhead pose with HDRI key
  aligned to the active-face direction so the rolled face is brightest;
  reads as a die at rest on a table viewed from above.
- File format: PNG with straight alpha (from Cycles transparent film).
- This is the free D20 sample. The full polyhedral set (D4-D20+D%) is
  available as the **Nightfall Marble Complete Polyhedral Result Sprite
  Pack** on itch.io.
- D20 sprites in the freebie are byte-identical to the D20 sprites in
  the full polyhedral pack; drop them in alongside the paid pack
  without any rewiring needed.
